Lover

Taylor Swift's 7th Studio Album As a Tribute to Love

Its Story

Released on August 23, 2019, Lover topped charts worldwide as Taylor's first album with Republic Records after departing her previous record label. The album received at least seven nominations and awards following its release, and you can still find some of the songs from the album trending on TikTok! See if you recognize any of them below!
